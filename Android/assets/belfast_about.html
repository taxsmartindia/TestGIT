Here is why we think our programme is the very best Trainee Programme available to you:

1. The Programme
  • Our Trainee Programme is designed to encourage and develop every person’s unique qualities to the maximum, it is an opportunity to discover what areas of law interest and engage you most
  • Rotations spent in our main departments to find out which areas suits and interests you best: Banking & Financial Services, Commercial Property, Corporate & Commercial, Employment, Litigation & Dispute Resolution and Restructuring & Insolvency
  • On-going Training and Education Programme, with in-house workshops, seminars and full access to our extensive research and library facilities. Financial assistance for relevant external courses

2. Client Secondment Opportunities
  • Training is structured to rotate around the core disciplines practiced in the Belfast office, but Trainees will also be given the opportunity to be seconded to key client teams in the banking and financial services sectors
  • In addition to client secondments, the Belfast office offers Trainee Solicitors the opportunity to be seconded to either our Dublin or London offices – yet another way of further developing your experience and skills set
3. Social Life
  • The Firm’s social committee organises a number of events throughout the year. Employees participate in Firm wide social and cultural activities as well as a number of sporting events
  • Team and departmental lunches, get-togethers and away days allow us to spend a little time with our colleagues outside of the work environment
  • Some of our social activities include: 5-a-side soccer team, Tag rugby, Calcutta Run, Christmas Party, Summer Barbecue and regular social get-togethers
