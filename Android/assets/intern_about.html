The Summer Internship Programme (SIP) forms an integral part of our resourcing strategy. Its importance is evidenced by the resources we dedicate to it throughout the Firm, including in particular, training and development. All of those who complete our Summer Internship Programme are automatically interviewed for our Trainee Programme in September of each year.

  • Four-week long, paid internships in our Dublin office during June and July of each year.
  • Applicants must have commenced either penultimate or final year undergraduate degree in the September preceding application, be post graduates or have successfully obtained a third level degree qualification.
  • Opportunity for you to learn from our legal professionals and for us to identify potential trainees
  • Insight into a future career in law.
  • Training and seminars from our top lawyers, award winning business support teams and external consultants on legal issues, the business of law, and areas of professional development.
