Here is why we think our programme is the very best Trainee Programme available to you: 1. Structure
  • We structure our trainee programme around 4 six month rotations or "seats"
  • Trainee seats are within the firm's main departments: Corporate, Banking & Financial Services, Litigation & Dispute Resolution, Commercial Property, Tax, and Pensions
  • Discover for yourself just where your interests and strengths lie
2. Mentoring
  • Trainees work within teams to provide structured mentoring from partners, associates and more junior lawyers
  • We want you to see how different people have a variety of styles and approaches to managing client work
  • Team mentoring shows how styles and skills develop at different stages in your legal career
3. Practical Feedback
  • Assessments every three months - mid. and end of seat assessments
  • Purpose is to give positive affirmation and to discuss any areas for improvement and work on them in the second half of the seat
4. Secondments & Seats in Other Offices
  • Secondment opportunities with clients across a range of industries. You will learn exactly what it is that our clients look for in their lawyers
  • Opportunity to complete a seat in our London or Belfast offices, to see what life is like in those cities and how client needs vary in different locations
  • Trainees on secondment / in other offices continue to receive trainee programme support and guidance
5. Legal Education & Learning
  • Education programme has three broad strands:
    • In house knowledge sharing presentations: Our top lawyers give trainees presentations on their areas of expertise, recent developments and upcoming developments in law in their area
    • Trainee Workshops: Depending on the department you are in, you will participate in a series of practical, partner led workshops which focus on the key practical skills required to practice in specific areas of law
    • Trainee Knowledge sharing: Led by the trainees themselves. Trainees simulate a client briefing or a client meeting where the other trainees act as clients in a real live meeting room scenario
6. Non-Legal Skills Development

In addition to our Trainee Education Programme, we provide seminar programmes and training sessions delivered by our award winning knowledge, business support and training teams here in A&L Goodbody, as well as external consultants, to give you all of the skills that you are going to need outside of pure legal knowledge
